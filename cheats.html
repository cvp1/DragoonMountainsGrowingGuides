The full prose for everything here lives in the seventeen guides — the cheats are the at-a-glance lookup. Source guides are noted in italics on each card. Elevation adjustment: shift dates 1–2 weeks earlier at 4,200–4,500 ft; 1–2 weeks later at 5,000–5,500 ft.
