Guide 16 · Design
Companion Plantings & Polycultures
The design layer for the library — not a crop guide but a how-the-crops-fit-together guide. Five biological mechanisms (repellence, attraction, allelopathy, nitrogen exchange, structural). A master matrix covering every cultivated crop × plant-with / plant-near / never-near. Cover crops for the Dragoons. Nine worked guild designs with sketch diagrams — three sisters in the Cochise context, pepper-basil-marigold, brassica-allium harlequin defense, stone-fruit orchard guild, fig + Mediterranean herbs, mesquite-anchored native guild, vineyard cover-crop interrow, continuous-bloom insectary strip, and a monsoon-fed three-bean polyculture.
0.6 MB · 64 pp · Rev. May 2026 Open
Guide 17 · Kitchen
Recipes & Kitchen Integration
The kitchen guide. What to cook with what came out of the garden this morning. Sixteen by-crop chapters — peppers, tomatoes, cucurbits, beans, corn, sweet potatoes, brassicas, alliums, greens, roots, grapes, herbs, orchard fruits, native crops — plus a "menus through the seasons" chapter with ten worked seasonal meals (January cold-frame greens, July monsoon tomato, September peak-harvest feast, Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve, New Year's). Around 80 recipes total. Cross-references *Guide 12* for any preserve-bound preparation; this guide is the fresh-eating daily-cooking counterpart.
